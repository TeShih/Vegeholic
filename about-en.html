About Vegeholic

Vegeholic is a brand aiming to promote a healthy plant-based lifestyle through recipe videos, speeches, seminars and cooking workshops.

Being vegan is not only a way of eliminating the consumption of animal products, it is above all a conscious lifestyle after learning about the cruel treatment of animals in the industry and its negative impact on the environment.

Through all the activities that we hold, including online recipe sharing, food and lifestyle seminars, and multi-cultural cooking workshops, we want people to know that a plant based diet is super fun and abundant! From fresh and colorful seasonal fruits and vegetables, fragrant spices to nutritious nuts and seeds, all of these wonderful foods provide us with the clean fuel that we need, in order to feel fresh and energized.
