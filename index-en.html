A Healthy, Sustainable and Mindful Lifestyle

Food and Health

The China Study, the most comprehensive study of nutrition ever conducted, has revealed that the closer people came to an all plant-based diet, the lower their risk for chronic disease.

Food and Sustainability

Animal agriculture is a major factor in climate change, water and air pollution, biodiversity loss and deforestation.

We all can contribute to the sustainability of our planet by choosing the right kinds of food.

Food and Mindfulness

124,000 animals are being slaughtered every minute for the food around the world.

Fuel your body with nutritious fruits and vegetables instead of painful body parts.
